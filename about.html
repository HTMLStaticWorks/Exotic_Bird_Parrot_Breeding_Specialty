Dedicated to Ethical Aviculture

What started as a small, passionate breeding interest in 2012 has evolved into a premier specialty aviary. We realized early on that many companion birds face behavioral and health challenges due to poor breeding practices and lack of early socialization. Our goal was to change that standard.

Our Commitments

Standard practices that set us apart as ethical breeders.

Health Guaranteed

Our Mission & Vision

We are dedicated to elevating the standard of aviculture and promoting responsible ownership for exotic birds.

Our Mission

To raise the healthiest, most stable, and well-socialized companion parrots, ensuring their seamless transition into educated, lifelong matching homes.

Our Vision

To be a globally trusted benchmark for ethical specialty aviculture, veterinary health alignment, and owner education, reducing the number of displaced birds.
