Our Adoption Process & Inclusions

Ensuring a smooth transition and lifetime support for your new companion parrot.

1. Health Guarantee

Every bird undergoes a complete veterinary physical assessment, bacterial check, and comes with a 1-year health guarantee.

2. DNA Sex Certification

Official DNA laboratory certificates verifying the gender and lineage of your bird are provided upon pickup.

3. Socialization Starter Kit

Includes a travel carrier, a 1-week supply of organic weaned pellets/chop, and a behavior guides resource pack.
