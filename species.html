Our Species & Care

Educating prospective owners on ethical husbandry and bird behaviors.

Lifelong Commitment

Adopting a Bird is a Journey

Parrots are highly intelligent, social, and emotional beings. Unlike typical domestic pets, many species can live for 30 to over 80 years. This longevity makes them a lifelong companion requiring immense dedication.

At Indus Aviary, we believe successful ownership starts with education. We nurture our chicks with high-quality diets and positive reinforcement socialization, preparing them for your home. However, it is vital to select a species whose noise, space, and intellectual demands match your lifestyle.

The Aviary Standard

  • Wean & Socialize: All chicks are hand-raised, fully socialized, and weaned to pellets and fresh foods.
  • Vet-Certified Health: Each bird receives standard DNA gender testing and a veterinary health certificate before adoption.
  • Lifetime Consulting: We provide ongoing support for dietary planning, behavior guidance, and training consultation.
